The best taquerías (taco restaurants) in CDMX



Let's begin with a fun fact: 94% of mexicans live at most 5 minutes away from a "taquería". So yeah, they're important for us.

Anyway, here's my ranking:

  1. THE ONE AND ONLY Tacos Charly
    2. They have simply the best "suadero" tacos in the world. I would advice to accompany them with a "Boing de guayaba" or "Boing de mango".
  2. Super Tacos Chupacabras
    3. The famous "taco chupas" is a mixture of many different types of meat and is quite tasty. You can also add an unlimited amount of sauce, potato purée, and vegetables for free.
  3. Orinoco
    4. It is always full of turists and kind of gentrified, but honestly they are extremely tasty.
  4. El Peri
    5. They add avocado and tamato to your tacos which is somewhat unusuall, but gives them an amazing taste. They are especially good after a party.
  5. Los Güeros del Ajusco
    6. Very traditional place and, as well as in Chupacabras, you can add lots of stuff for free, which makes them a huge deal.
  6. El Paisa de los Reyes
    7. Just delicious.
  7. El Villamelón
    8. Peak meat quality. Somewhat more expensive, though.
  8. El Borrego Viudo
    9. Quite good and you can order them and eat them in your car. They are famous for having the quickest waiters in the city. (They've been involved in some scandals in the past few years, though).
  9. Don Manolito
    10. This is a chain of taco places and they are quite expensive, but the "maja" (beef taco inside a cheese shell) is very good and quite original.
  10. El Vilsito
    11. In the morning it is a mechanical workshop and in the afternoon it is a taco place. For that reason they might not be the best if you're too picky about hygene, but they're delicious anyways.
  11. Los Milanesos
    12. Very good "milanesa tacos". They are on the street and they are not the most hygenic, in case you're picky.
  12. El Charco de Las Ranas
    13. Very famous taco chain in Mexico. In my opinion they are quite expensive but their quality and taste is great.
