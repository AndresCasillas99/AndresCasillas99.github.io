I'm a mexican mathematician living in Bonn, Germany. I completed my M.Sc. in Mathematics with a minor in Computer Science at Bonn University. I defended my thesis on Machine Learning Algorithmic Optimization with the highest grade, which I wrote under the supervision of Prof. Dr. Marc Alexander Schweitzer at Bonn's Institute fur Numerical Simulation. I'm currently a student research assistant at Fraunhofer Institute for Algorithms and Scientific Computing.

Previously, I completed my B.Sc. in Mathematics with honors at UNAM and was awarded the "Sotero Prieto 2024" medal by the Mexican Mathematical Society for the best mathematics thesis in Mexico, as well as the "Gabino Barreda" diploma for achieving the second-highest GPA in my class.

My main interests lie in data science (particularly applied to sports), machine learning, and everything that has to do with using algorithms smartly (most of the time with lots of heavy math behind them).
