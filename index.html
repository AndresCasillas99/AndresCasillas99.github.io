I am currently a master’s student in mathematics at Bonn University
and a student research assistant at Fraunhofer Institute for Algorithms and Scientific Computing.

I wrote my bachelor´s thesis on a very interesting connection between cellular automata and number theory, for which I was granted the «Sotero Prieto 2024» medal by the Mexican Mathematical Society for the best undergraduate thesis in mathematics in Mexico.

My main interests lie in computability theory, number theory, mathematical logic, and discrete dynamical systems.
