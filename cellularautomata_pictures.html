Some nice cellular automata :)


One can codify linear cellular automata with polynomials. I've computed many of such cellular automata and I'm pleased to share them here, accompanied by their "evolution's polynomial".

  • Pascal's Triangle mod m for m = 2,...,41
  • Dynamical cellular automata
  • Generalizations of Pascal's Triangle
  • Langton's Ant
  • Conway's Game of Life (Julia)
  • Conway's Game of Life (Processing)
