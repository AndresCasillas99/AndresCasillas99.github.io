I'm a mexican mathematician currently doing a master’s in mathematics at Bonn University
and a student research assistant at Fraunhofer Institute for Algorithms and Scientific Computing.

I'm writting my master's thesis on Parallel Algebraic Multigrid Methods (AMG) under the supervision of Prof. Dr. Marc Alexander Schweitzer at Bonn's Institute fur Numerical Simulation.

Previously I was a scholar at IMATE, UNAM (Institute of Mathematics of the National Autonomous University of Mexico) and a bachelor's student in mathematics at UNAM´s Faculty of Science in Mexico City, Mexico.

I wrote my bachelor´s thesis on a very interesting connection between cellular automata and number theory, for which I was granted the «Sotero Prieto 2024» medal by the Mexican Mathematical Society for the best undergraduate thesis in mathematics in Mexico.

My main interests lie in computability theory, numerics (numerical lineal algebra, numerical analysis, numerical number theory), and dynamical systems.
